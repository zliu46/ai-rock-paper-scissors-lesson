How to use

  1. Train a Teachable Machine image model with classes named Rock, Paper, and Scissors.
  2. Export it as a TensorFlow.js model and copy its share URL.
  3. Paste the URL above and click Load Model & Start.
  4. Show a gesture to the webcam and click Play Round.
  5. The match ends when either side reaches 3 wins.
